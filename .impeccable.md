# Design Context — Lalla Fatema

## Target audience
Moroccan & Arabic-speaking women. Mobile-first, fast scrollers who come for celebrity/entertainment news and stay for fashion, beauty, health, lifestyle, recipes, bride content, and video. Reading right-to-left (Arabic).

## Use cases
- Skim the latest celebrity/entertainment headlines fast (feed energy).
- Browse by category; read image-rich articles; watch videos; browse numbered magazine issues; share to social.

## Brand personality / tone
Premium, glossy, feminine lifestyle magazine. Image-forward and dense like **foochia.com** (younger, entertainment-feed, lots of cards, varied sizes, wide use of the viewport with tight gutters), with the editorial polish and sectioned curation of **layalina.com**. Confident and warm, trustworthy — not spammy or cluttered. The Video section is visually distinct/highlighted (darker or tinted background).

## Visual language
- **Brand accent:** magenta `#bc0168` (`brand-600`) + tints. White/near-white background, near-black ink (tinted zinc). Tint neutrals subtly toward the magenta.
- **Type:** Tajawal (Arabic, self-hosted). Strong weight contrast for hierarchy. RTL everywhere; Tailwind logical properties (ps/pe/ms/me, start/end).
- **Layout:** wide content (large max-width, tight side gutters), dense magazine grid with **varied block sizes** (lead + secondary + list), not uniform cards. Break the grid intentionally. Section headings with a magenta accent bar (start side) + "عرض الكل".
- **Cards:** image-forward, category kicker + headline + relative time; minimal chrome (no heavy drop-shadows / nested cards).
- **Highlighted Video section:** dark/tinted band that contrasts with the white feed.

## Constraints
Next.js 16 (App Router) + Tailwind v4, Payload CMS. RTL Arabic only. Performance: priority LCP hero, zero-CLS reserved aspect ratios, minimal client JS (good INP). Accessibility (semantic, keyboard, contrast, reduced-motion). Images NEVER use Vercel's optimizer — custom Cloudflare loader only.
